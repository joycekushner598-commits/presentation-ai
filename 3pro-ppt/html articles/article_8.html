The Material Truth

This is where practical care comes into play. This isn't high-end jewelry, so proper maintenance is essential. I follow these steps religiously:

  1. Apply perfume and lotion first, allowing them to fully dry
  2. Put on the jewelry afterward
  3. Remove immediately upon returning home
  4. Store in a dry bag away from humidity

Don't trust marketing claims about water resistance. If you treat this set with care, it can last for months. However, exposure to rain or shower water will cause rapid fading. Understand the limitations before purchasing.
